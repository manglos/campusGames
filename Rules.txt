You Can't Hide Rules:

Overview-
You Can't hide is a game derived from the childhood game "Hide and seek." The intention of the game is that 3 or more people get together to play a game where some hide and some look for the people hiding. 

Players-
It: a person who is designated to look for the Runners and try to tag them before they get to the Home Base or before time runs out. The It can tag more than one person in a round and will get bonus experience

Spectator:

Field of play-
A game of You Can't Hide. in general, has a bounded playing area. Players must stay within that area in order to play. The area for the Home Base is determined by the host of the game, and is normally has a three-foot diameter.
